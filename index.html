Tips to stretch your budget
Public transport passes are often much cheaper than owning a car — check if your destination has a flat-rate transit ticket.
Budget supermarkets (like Aldi/Lidl in Germany) offer quality groceries at 30–40% less than premium stores.
Open a local bank account with zero fees immediately — N26, Revolut, or country-specific digital banks often have English apps.
